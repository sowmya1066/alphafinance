Top Services

We offer various types of loans tailored to your needs. These may include personal loans, home loans (mortgages), auto loans, student loans, business loans. The interest rate can be fixed or variable, depending on the loan agreement. The rate is often influenced by factors such as the borrower's creditworthiness, prevailing market rates, and the type of loan.

Net banking allows you to pay bills electronically, including utilities, credit cards, loans, and other expenses. Users can schedule one-time or recurring payments, helping them to manage their finances more efficiently.

Certificates of Deposit (CDs) are fixed-income investments offered by us. When an investor purchases a CD, they deposit a specific amount of money with the bank for a predetermined period, known as the term. In return, the bank pays the investor interest at a fixed rate.

Savings accounts are deposit accounts designed to help customers save money over time. Depositors can earn interest on the funds deposited into a savings account, although the interest rates tend to be lower compared to other types of accounts. Savings accounts typically offer easy access to funds, allowing depositors to make withdrawals or transfers as needed.
